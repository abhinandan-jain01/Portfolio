Winter Survival
The Hunt for Tomorrow

3D open-world exploration survival game built for the Digifest Hackathon, Jaipur. Navigate a harsh winter wilderness, gather food, evade intelligent predators, and protect your family.

🌍 Open-World Exploration 🌨️ Day-Night Cycle 🐺 Predator AI 🍖 Quota System 🎵 Adaptive Sound

About

Originally developed for the Digifest Hackathon in Jaipur (Top 10 Finish). You play as a lone family man forced to venture into a frozen open-world forest each day. With rising daily quotas and intelligent wolves, every decision matters — run, distract, or sacrifice food to escape.

Key Systems

  • 🌍 Open-World Exploration – Freely explorable winter environment with level layout and asset integration
  • 🌨️ Day-Night Cycle – Dynamic lighting transforms the environment
  • 🐺 Predator AI – Wolves track movement and adjust behavior
  • 🍖 Survival Quota – Escalating daily food requirements
  • ❤️ Health System – Consequence-based survival mechanics
  • 🎵 Adaptive Soundtrack – Music reacts to danger dynamically
  • ❄️ Atmosphere – Designed to evoke isolation and urgency
